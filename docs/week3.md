# Volcanoes

## Purpose

As part of this Earth Science course, you will complete a course-long project. You will research the geophysical processes of a country of your choice (for example, your birth country or a country for which you have close ties). Up through Week 6 of the block, you will work on different segments of this assignment and add high-quality, relevant content to a website that you will create. At the end of Week 6, you will have a completed website detailing the Earth’s internal and external geographic processes and how the physical geography of the region impacted the development of your country. As you complete this project you will develop soft skills that will be beneficial to your future career.

## Instructions

-   **PART A: WEBSITE BUILDING - DUE BY SATURDAY @ 11:59 PM!**

    This week, we have discussed plate tectonics, the rock cycle, earthquakes, and volcanoes. For this second segment of the course-long assignment, you will be researching the following questions about your country and including information on your web page about the following:  

    **Tectonic Plates and Natural Disasters**: 

    -   Is your country near tectonic plate boundaries? Describe the type and location of the boundaries. 

    -   Does your country experience natural disasters like earthquakes, volcanoes, or tsunamis? Provide specific examples. 

    **Rock and Mineral Resources**: 

    -   What types of rocks or minerals are found in your country, and how do they benefit the economy? 

    -   How do mineral resources affect jobs and exports? Where are they exported to? 

    -   Are there other impacts from these resources (e.g., environmental, economic)? 

    **Earthquakes**: 

    -   Does your country experience significant earthquakes? Give historical examples. 

    -   What is the government doing to help citizens prepare for earthquakes? 

    **Mountain Ranges**: 

    -   Which major mountain ranges are in or near your country? Describe each (structure, elevation, etc.). 

    -   How do these mountains affect weather, water supplies, and local culture? 

    -   Did they provide protection or impact trade routes, both historically and now? 

    **Volcanoes**: 

    -   Does your country have significant volcanoes? List them and describe their type (shield, composite, etc.). 

    -   How do volcanoes impact the region (fertile soil, tourism, threats)? 

    -   Give historical examples of eruptions and their effects. 

    Include a video, podcast, interview, pictures, charts, or graphs to bring your research to life.

    Make sure to cite your reputable sources, using proper APA formatting. (Note: Using artificial intelligence (AI) as a springboard for deeper research can be beneficial. However, it is a tool, not a substitution for good research. Direct copying and pasting from AI websites is unacceptable. Always back up your findings with high-quality sources, and be sure to cite your use of AI properly.) 

    Update your website with this information and re-post the link to your website here in Canvas.
