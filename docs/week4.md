## Purpose

As part of this Earth Science course, you will complete a course-long project. You will research the geophysical processes of a country of your choice (for example, your birth country or a country for which you have close ties). Up through Week 6 of the block, you will work on different segments of this assignment and add high-quality, relevant content to a website that you will create. At the end of Week 6, you will have a completed website detailing the Earth’s internal and external geographic processes and how the physical geography of the region impacted the development of your country. As you complete this project you will develop soft skills that will be beneficial to your future career.

## Instructions

**PART A: WEBSITE BUILDING - DUE BY SATURDAY @ 11:59 PM!**

This week, we have discussed weathering, mass wasting, and erosion. For this next segment of your course-long country assignment, you will research the following questions about your country and include information on your web page about the following:  

**Weathering and Mass Wasting**: 

-   How does your country experience weathering (like carbonation and hydrolysis) and mass wasting (e.g., landslides)? 

-   Include at least one example of a major event in the past and what the government has done to reduce these risks. 

**Water Erosion:**

Some water erosion processes are very localized and may not have a huge bearing on the way your country functions economically/socially, etc. Some examples of these processes might be splash, sheet, inter-rill, rill, gully erosion, and streambank erosion. However, erosion along major rivers can have big impacts on a regional scale.

-   Provide specific examples of major rivers in your selected country and give details about their sources regions, lengths, discharges, estuaries/deltas, etc.
-   Do they have straight/braided/meandering river channels? Explain how river erosion impacts the communities along these rivers economically and socially.
-   What steps is the government taking to mitigate for river erosion?

**Wind Erosion:**

-   Does your country experience wind erosion? If so, what is your country's government doing to mitigate it's effects?

**Deserts and Desertification**: 

-   Do deserts affect your country? How? 

-   List the deserts and describe each, including their landscapes (erg, reg, or hamada). 

-   Is desertification becoming a problem? 

**Glaciers**: 

-   Do glaciers (both current and ancient) affect your country? 
-   How do any remaining glacial features impact the country? 

Include a video, podcast, interview, pictures, charts, or graphs to bring your research to life.

**Make sure to cite your reputable sources, using proper APA formatting. (Note: Using artificial intelligence (AI) as a springboard for deeper research can be beneficial. Always back up your findings with high-quality sources, and be sure to cite your use of AI properly.)**
