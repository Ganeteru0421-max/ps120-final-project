# Coastlines

## Purpose

As part of this Earth Science course, you will complete a course-long project. You will research the geophysical processes of a country of your choice (for example, your birth country or a country for which you have close ties). Up through Week 6 of the block, you will work on different segments of this assignment and add high-quality, relevant content to a website that you will create. At the end of Week 6, you will have a completed website detailing the Earth’s internal and external geographic processes and how the physical geography of the region impacted the development of your country. As you complete this project you will develop soft skills that will be beneficial to your future career.

## Instructions

**PART A: WEBSITE BUILDING - DUE BY SATURDAY @ 11:59 PM!**

This week, we have discussed fluvial processes, oceans and coastal environments. For this next segment of the course-long assignment, you will be researching the following questions about your country and including information on your web page about the following:  

**Flooding**: 

-   Is flooding an issue? Where does it happen, and how often? 

-   What steps is the government taking to prepare for floods? 

**Land and River Trade Routes**: 

-   Which major trade routes (land and river) pass through your country, and how does geology affect them?
-   What are the advantages and disadvantages of these trade routes?
-   Who are your country’s main trading partners? Share statistics on the most important import and export countries.

**Oceans, Ocean Trade Routes & Islands:**

-   Describe any important ocean currents and their effects on your country’s climate.
-   How does your country’s proximity to the ocean impact it (e.g., climate, hurricane risks, navigation)?
-   Which oceans and shipping routes affect your country, both historically and now?
-   If landlocked, how does your country transport goods to the ocean for trade?
-   Does your country have islands? How do they impact the economy and culture? 

**Coastlines**: 

-   Does your country have a coastline? Describe its shape (regular or irregular) and composition (rocky, sandy, etc.). 

-   How do the coastline’s features affect your country (positively or negatively)? 

 **Hurricanes and Tsunamis**: 

-   Has your country experienced hurricanes or tsunamis? Provide historical examples. 

Include a video, podcast, interview, pictures, charts, or graphs to bring your research to life.

Make sure to cite your reputable sources, using proper APA formatting. (Note: Using artificial intelligence (AI) as a springboard for deeper research can be beneficial. However, it is a tool, not a substitution for good research. Direct copying and pasting from AI websites is unacceptable. Always back up your findings with high-quality sources, and be sure to cite your use of AI properly.)
