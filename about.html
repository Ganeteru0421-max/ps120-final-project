My ties to Japan

I have family background in Japan. I have visited Kyoto and Niseko in Hokkaido. I picked Japan for this project so I can learn how the land and oceans work there.

One fact about the land

Japan gets many earthquakes and volcanoes. That happens because huge plates of rock under the ocean push under Japan. The country also has steep mountains and long coastlines.
