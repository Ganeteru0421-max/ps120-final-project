# Projections

## Purpose

As part of this Earth Science course, you will complete a course-long project. You will research the geophysical processes of a country of your choice (for example, your birth country or a country for which you have close ties). Up through Week 6 of the block, you will work on different segments of this assignment and add high-quality, relevant content to a website that you will create. At the end of Week 6, you will have a completed website detailing the Earth’s internal and external geographic processes and how the physical geography of the region impacted the development of your country. As you complete this project you will develop soft skills that will be beneficial to your future career.

##  Instructions

-   **PART A: WEBSITE BUILDING - DUE BY SATURDAY @ 11:59 PM!**

    Now that you have set up your web page, it’s time to include some high-quality content. The first page you will include are maps of your country.  On your web page, include at least four maps that add to our understanding of your country along with a sentence or two giving some context of what each map shows. For example, you could include the following:   

    -   Ancient maps 

    -   Trade routes, sea voyage routes, warpaths, and interactions between your country and other countries, both past and present 

    -   A map showing the continental position and latitudinal range of your country  

    -   Modern maps (state what the map projection is)  

    -   Maps showing your country’s boundary changes over time  

    Also include written answers to the following four questions:  

    -   On which continent and in what general area is your country located?  

    -   What is the latitudinal range of your country?   

    -   During what time periods or years has your country existed?   

    -   Are there any famous cartographers from your country or cartographers from other countries who influenced map making in your country? If so, provide a brief history and a photo or image. What were their biggest cartographic accomplishments?  

    Make sure to properly cite your reputable sources, using proper APA formatting. (Note: Using artificial intelligence (AI) as a springboard for deeper research can be beneficial. However, it is a tool, not a substitution for good research. Direct copying and pasting from AI websites is unacceptable. Always back up your findings with high-quality sources and be sure to cite your use of AI properly.) 

    Update your website with this information and re-post the link to your website here in Canvas.
